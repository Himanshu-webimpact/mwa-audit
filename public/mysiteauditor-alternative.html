My Web Audit Vs. MySiteAuditor:

Let's get one thing out of the way

MySiteAuditor's SEO Audit Tool was launched in 2013. It's a helpful tool if you're looking for standardized automated on-page SEO reports, but not so much if you want something more customized and comprehensive that includes the types of reports agencies need to run to be successful in 2023.

VS

My Web Audit helps close not only SEO projects, but projects dealing with website design and redesign, ecommerce, conversion, Google Business Profile, and more... With a wide range of attractive, actionable audits written in consumer-focused language. White-label anything you want—including audit questions and responses—and get help any time you need.

Want to talk about more than SEO?
You'll need more than MySiteAuditor

Business owners have a hard time comprehending a typical SEO audit. Because, frankly, the topics you need to talk about with a lead when you're discussing SEO are just too technical for most people to understand and act on.

That makes MySiteAuditor more of a specialized tool. It uncovers many helpful insights, but you'll need to be prepared to "dumb them down" and do a lot of extra work to deliver something that's of value to your lead.

But by moving past rigid SEO audits and embracing other audits as well—like web design, conversion effectiveness, and e-commerce—My Web Audit gives you more ways to start a sales conversation and deliver more value.

My Web Audit reports cover everything important to today's marketing-focused business owners: user experience, conversion optimization, performance and security, SEO and online visibility, copy & content, and more.

And because these reports are so beautiful and professional-looking, you can show them anywhere you want to impress. Sales calls, quarterly and annual meetings, webinars—My Web Audit reports will make a massive impact when you want to demonstrate you can generate revenue and ROI.

As a result, you'll run rings around the competition as you demonstrate value with a pitch that's about more than "just" SEO.
